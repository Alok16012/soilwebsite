About SOIL

Transforming Agriculture Through Nature, Science & Sustainability

At SOIL, healthy soil is the foundation of healthy food, healthy farmers, and a healthy nation. We restore soil health, empower farmers, and create sustainable farm-to-market opportunities.

Welcome to SOIL

Restoring soil for a stronger agricultural future

SOIL (Sheevar Organic Industrial Limited) promotes sustainable farming through bio-fertilizers, bio-inputs, organic farming solutions, and assured market linkages for farmers. Our mission is to reduce dependence on harmful chemical fertilizers and pesticides while improving soil fertility, crop productivity, and farmer income.

Our Promise

From soil health management to organic crop procurement, SOIL stands beside farmers throughout the journey.
