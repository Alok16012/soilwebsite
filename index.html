About Us

Restoring Soil. Empowering Farmers.

At SOIL (Sheevar Organic Industrial Limited), healthy soil is the foundation of healthy food, healthy farmers, and a healthy nation. We support Indian agriculture with sustainable bio-products, organic farming guidance, and long-term farmer partnerships.

  • Promote eco-friendly and sustainable agriculture
  • Improve soil fertility through advanced bio-based products
  • Support farmers in transitioning from chemical farming to organic farming
  • Build transparent farm-to-market support and long-term prosperity
